## Resources

- Official Cisco CCNA page: https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/training-events/training-certifications/certifications/associate/ccna.html
- Official CCNA exam blueprint (use to scope study): https://learningnetwork.cisco.com/s/ccna-exam-topics
- Recommended books:
	- "CCNA 200-301 Official Cert Guide" (Cisco Press)
	- "31 Days Before Your CCNA Exam" (Syngress)
- Labs & simulators:
	- Cisco Packet Tracer (education-focused simulator)
	- GNS3 (full-featured emulator)
	- Boson NetSim (paid, high-quality CCNA labs)
- Courses & videos:
	- Cisco Learning Network
	- CBT Nuggets, INE, Pluralsight (paid courses)
	- NetworkChuck, David Bombal (YouTube) for practical labs and explanations
- Practice & tools:
	- Subnetting practice: https://www.ipcalc.org/ or https://www.subnetonline.com/
	- IPv4/IPv6 calculators and CIDR tools
	- Packet capture: Wireshark (for protocol analysis)

See `RESOURCES.md` for a curated list and quick links.
