Books

- 31 Days Before Your CCNA Exam (Syngress)

Practice & Tools
- Subnetting practice: https://www.subnetonline.com/ or https://www.ipcalc.org/
- Wireshark (packet analysis): https://www.wireshark.org/
- IPv4/IPv6 calculators / CIDR tools: search for "CIDR calculator"

Study tips
- Follow the official exam blueprint and make a checklist of topics.
- Build hands-on labs early: practice subnetting, VLANs, OSPF basics, and NAT in Packet Tracer or GNS3.
- Timebox study sessions and keep a lab log of configurations you used.
